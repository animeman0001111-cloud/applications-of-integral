üìò What is the Center of Mass?

The center of mass of a plane object (lamina) is the point at which the entire mass of the object can be considered to be concentrated.

For uniform lamina, the center of mass depends only on the shape, not on thickness or density.

üìè Center of Mass of Uniform Lamina

If the lamina has uniform density, then:

  • Mass is proportional to area
  • Centroid coincides with center of mass
  • Integration simplifies significantly

For symmetric regions, the center of mass lies on the axis of symmetry.
